Middle East International School

Middle East International School was founded in 1998, to serve the educational needs of the expatriate student community of Riyadh. Serving grades KGs to 12, the school aims to provide a high standard of education to a multicultural, multilingual group of students. Since its inception, the school has emerged as one of the leading educational institutions in Riyadh.
